About Us

At Infocap Securities Pvt. Ltd., we believe that financial empowerment is not a privilege—it's a right. Founded with the vision of making intelligent investing accessible to every individual, we are a dynamic financial services and technology company headquartered in Madurai, India.

Our core strengths lie in three pillars: Algorithmic Trading Solutions, Portfolio Management Services (PMS), and Comprehensive Share Market Education. We are the proud creators of Best Algo AI, a next-generation trading software that enables traders to automate strategies and manage risks like professionals.

What sets us apart is not just our technology, but the human-driven approach behind it. Every product and service we deliver is deeply rooted in research, market insight, and most importantly, the needs of our clients.

Whether you're a retail trader seeking smart automation, an investor looking for expert portfolio management, or a student eager to learn the art of stock market trading—we have built a platform for you.
